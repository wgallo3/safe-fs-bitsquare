Listing on Bitsquare

Pursuing partnering with Distributed Exchange

SAFE-FS seeks listing on Bitsquare

After making very limited progress with centralized exchanges such as Poloniex, Bittrex, Yuanbao, Bleutrade, and Yobit, our small project is looking towards the more decentralized exchanges for ways for our users to trade their SAFE-FS Coins with the world. While private escrow deals have happened with SFSC already, and are a very viable short-term method, listing with exchanges is obviously preferred and continues to be pursued.

Bitsquare is a decentralized Bitcoin exchange, that has built-in methods for exchanging evenly into various fiat currencies through a variety of methods, but also offers exchanging for altcoins (MAID currently supported) which is what interests our project.

We have reviewed their requirements listed here, and have created a public request which can be viewed here. It would be amazing to be listed with such a secure, resilient exchange that mirrors most if not all of the core principles of SAFE, and all community support is needed to help make this happen! So please create a Bitsquare Forum account here and support our efforts by replying to the thread here, showing your desire for Bitsquare to add SFSC!

Distributed Exchange

Partnering with Bitsquare has many benefits over traditional, centralized exchanges, in that trading can never be shut down or blocked in any way, and neither party needs to forfeit any unnessecary information.

Sleek User Interface

In the small world of clearnet distributed exchanges, Bitsquare truly stands out due to its obvious dedication to the user experience and adoptability of its platform; important benefits to weigh when deciding.

Decentralized Support

Bitsquare offers support and help in a decentralized fashion, as anyone can sign up and gain rating as a trade moderator, who earns a fee for providing a safely escrowed trade. It reflects many principles of SAFE.
