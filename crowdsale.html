SAFE-FS Crowdsale Overview

The Crowdsale for SAFE-FS Coin (SFSC) was open for a total of three weeks, beginning at 12am, the first minute of Wednesday June 29th '16, and end at 12pm (midnight 24:00), the the final minute of Tuesday July 19th '16. 5,000,000 SFSC has since been issued in an even proportion to the addresses that sent timely payments to the BTC / MAID addresses. This is our only SAFE coin eligible for exchanges.

Funds raised go directly towards the development, marketing and outreach of the SAFE File System (SAFE-FS). While we could not predict the funding raised in this sale, we were able to achieve all of our specific funding goals, with the extra going directly to marketing and outreach efforts for SAFE-FS, SFSC, and, indirectly, our foundations in the SAFE Network.
