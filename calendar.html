Project Marketing & Outreach

A vigorous campaign of Marketing and Presentations will happen alongside SAFE-FS development. In addition, to support our investors, we will work to get SAFE-FS Coin (SFSC) on as many exchanges as possible (with Bitsquare's decentralized platform as our top pick currently). This gives us a responsibility to ensure the message of our project is adequately communicated to the world. Any extra time we have between releases of SAFE Network API's / features are perfect opportunities for us to present for our project, and for the SAFE Network.

We have already begun organizing and implementing an aggresive marketing campaign for our project and the network, to best utilize this time before the SAFE API's for our app are released. There are many relevant Meetups and free events in our area, however with proper funding we will be able to hire multiple presenters and offer more powerful presentations, to multiply our efforts and outreach. This could prove instrumental in getting the message of SAFE-FS and of the SAFE Network out to a myriad of future users / investors.

Due to the levels of funding raised in the sale, we are able to present very often, from a minimum of a few presentations every month to much more, depending on the availability of local audiences. All crowdsale investors have input into which events we will attend in our area.

Listing on Exchanges

There are many coin Exchanges that we are looking to list SFSC on, including Poloniex, Bittrex and Yuanbao. We have reviewed all of their application processes and begun organizing ourselves for them.

Establishing Value

In order to establish and maintain value for our project, we need to always keep fresh marketing materials and present at various types of events. This approach could be multiplied by hiring additional presenters.

Powerful Marketing

SAFE-FS is a combination of expedient development and large outreach marketing. It is important to bring the core message of our project: privacy, security & freedom of your files, to as many audiences as possible.
